How it works

From brain dump to clarity in four steps.

1

Clear your head

Write down everything swirling in your head. No structure needed. Just let it out.

2

Set your context

Tell us your time, energy level, and focus area for the week. Takes 10 seconds.

3

Get your plan

AI scores and prioritises your tasks. You get realistic priorities plus what you're parking.

4

Work through it

Check off tasks as you go. See your progress. Choose which parked tasks to carry into next week.
